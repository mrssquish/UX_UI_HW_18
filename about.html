About Me

My name is Cassie Meyers, I’m a User Experience Designer hailing from the Maryland-Washington Dc area. I live from project to project to fulfill a deep seeded passion for design. I believe in research, empathy and ingenuity when it comes to design, and work hard to employ these key values in my work.

I design for users to interact with and enjoy. I design to align function with creativity and beauty. I design to reduce chaos and create ease.

When I’m not designing, you can catch me spending time with my amazing family. I enjoy camping, cooking five star meals, embarking in construction projects and baking cakes.
